Pi Day 2021

This project was a small celebration of Pi Day on 3/14/2021. It illustrates the limiting behavior of Archimedes method pf approximating pi, which involves calculating the circumference of a regular polygon inscribed in a circle. As the number of sides increases, the circumference approaches that of the circle, from which pi can be extracted.
